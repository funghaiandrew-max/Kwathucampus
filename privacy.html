Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 5, 2026

Kwathu Campus is operated by @Lost Loyalty Development. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect data needed to run the platform:

  • Account Data: Name, email address, phone number for Landlords. Students browse without an account.
  • Hostel Data: Hostel name, university, location, price, room type, and photos you upload.
  • Device Data: Your selected university, stored in your browser via `localStorage` to remember your choice.
  • Usage Data: Basic logs like IP address and pages viewed, collected by Supabase for security.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To show your hostel listings to students searching by university.
  • To allow students to contact landlords via WhatsApp. We do not see your chat.
  • To manage your account, prevent fraud, and improve Kwathu Campus.
  • To save requests for universities that are not on our list yet.

3. Data Storage & Security

All data is stored securely on Supabase, a cloud database provider. Photos are stored in Supabase Storage. We use Row Level Security `RLS` so Landlords can only view or edit their own hostels and account. No one else can access it.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We only share:

  • Public Listings: Your hostel name, location, price, and photos are public to all students.
  • Service Providers: Supabase, who host our database and files under strict data agreements.
  • Legal: If required by Malawi law or court order.

5. Photos & Content

By uploading photos, you confirm you own them or have permission. We may remove photos that are fake, offensive, or do not show the actual hostel.

6. Your Rights - Malawi Data Protection Act

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To request this, email us via the Contact link below. Landlords can delete their account from the Dashboard. This removes your data within 30 days.

7. Cookies & Local Storage

We use `localStorage` in your browser to remember your selected university. This is not shared with us and stays on your device. No tracking cookies are used.

8. Third-Party Links

Kwathu links to WhatsApp for communication. We are not responsible for WhatsApp’s privacy practices. Use at your own risk.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "Last Updated" date above will change. Continued use means you accept the changes.
